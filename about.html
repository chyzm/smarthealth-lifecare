Our Story

From humble beginnings to healthcare excellence

2018

Foundation

SmartHealth LifeCare group was founded by Dr. Sam Ogboo with a single clinic in Abijo, Lagos. It is a multi-specialty practice tailored to suit the health needs of our patients.

We focus on evidence based and specialist backed practice.

2019

Expansion

Due to growing demand for our unique kind of practice, we expanded our locations to Ikota, Lekki, Victoria Island, Ikoyi and Ikeja to meet the growing demand in these locations.

2020

Technology Integration

Implemented electronic health records and launched our telemedicine platform, expanding access to care across Nigeria.

2022

State-of-the-Art Facility

Our locations are all state-of-the-art medical centers with advanced diagnostic departments and a full range of specialty services.

Today

Continuing Excellence

Serving over 5,000 patients annually with a team of 20+ specialists across an array of medical disciplines.

Meet Our Expert Medical Team

Board-certified specialists dedicated to your health

Dr. Sam Ogboo

Founder & Medical Director

Board-certified physician with 10+ years experience in healthcare leadership. Specializes in internal medicine and healthcare administration.

Dr. Ogboo founded SmartHealth LifeCare in 2015 with a vision to create a patient-centered healthcare facility. He completed his residency at Lagos University Teaching Hospital and holds an MBA in Healthcare Management. His research focuses on improving healthcare access in urban communities.

Dr David JesuDemi

Senior Obstetrician & Gynaecologist

Dr David JesuDemi (MBBS Ib,MWACS(OBS & Gynae)) is a resourceful specialist that has been with our team for over 1 year.

He demonstrates dexterity in fine gynaecological surgeries such fibroid excision surgery, breast lump excisions,acute management of ectopic pregnancies, caesarean sections,specialist supervision of vaginal deliveries and all sorts of family planning. He is subspecialised in fertility gynaecological interventions such as IUI, egg harvesting & freezing and gynaecological tumour management. He manages our Ikeja & sometimes Lekki SmartHealth LifeCare OBS & Gynae practice.

Dr. Amina Yusuf

Head of Pediatrics

Pediatric specialist with expertise in neonatal care and childhood development disorders. Advocate for vaccination programs.

Dr. Emeka Okoro
